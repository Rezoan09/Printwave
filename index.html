📋 1. Definitions

"AnyPrint" refers to the company operating this platform. "Client" refers to the business or individual placing an order. "Goods" means the custom apparel produced under the Order. "Production Day" means any working day (Mon–Sat) at our Bangladesh facility. "Delivery Window" means the 21–25 calendar day period from Order Confirmation to delivery at the Client's US address.

📦 2. Order Placement & MOQ
⚠️ Minimum Order Quantity: 50 pieces per design per product type. Orders below this threshold cannot be accepted.

An order is confirmed only upon: (a) completed order form with full specs, (b) final approved design files (AI/PDF/PNG 300dpi+), (c) 50% deposit cleared, (d) written approval of digital proof. AnyPrint reserves the right to reject orders containing offensive, discriminatory, or infringing content.

💳 3. Payment Terms
💡 50% deposit to begin production + 50% balance before shipment release.

Accepted: Bank wire (ACH/SWIFT), PayPal Business, Wise. Credit cards accepted at 3% surcharge. All prices in USD, valid 14 days from quote. Balance due within 5 business days of shipment-ready notice; late payment incurs $15/day storage. All transfer fees are the Client's responsibility.

🎨 4. Design Files & Intellectual Property

By submitting files, the Client warrants ownership or license for all artwork, logos, and text. The Client indemnifies AnyPrint against IP infringement claims. Files are used solely for the Client's order. A digital proof is sent within 48–72 hours; production starts only after written approval.

🖨️ Files must be vector (AI/PDF) or 300dpi+ PNG. AnyPrint is not responsible for quality issues from low-resolution file submissions.
🏭 5. Production & Quality Control

Standard production: 8–12 production days post confirmation. Rush (5–7 days) at 20% surcharge. Industry defect tolerance: up to 3% — exceeded items are replaced or credited. Color variance up to 10–15% from digital mockups is normal. Size tolerance: ±0.5 inches is standard. Pre-shipment photo reports for Growth and Scale orders.

🚢 6. Shipping & Delivery
📅 The 21–25 day window starts from Order Confirmation — after design approval + deposit cleared — not from form submission.

Standard: sea freight to US ports (LA, Miami, NY, Houston). Air freight optional, 10–14 days at extra cost. Included: sea freight, origin handling, export docs. NOT included: US customs duties, ISF filing, last-mile delivery unless stated. Risk transfers to Client at FOB Chittagong/Dhaka.

↩️ 7. Returns & Claims

Claims must be submitted within 14 calendar days of delivery with photo evidence. Remedies: replacement or credit equal to defective unit cost. Returns NOT accepted for change of mind, slow sales, incorrect size selection, or color variation within tolerance.

🚫 Custom-manufactured goods are non-refundable once production has commenced.
❌ 8. Cancellations & Modifications

  • After proof approval, before production: 25% of total order value forfeited.
  • During production (Days 1–6): 50% forfeited.
  • After production completion: 100% forfeited; goods shipped at Client's cost.
⚖️ 9. Limitation of Liability

AnyPrint's total liability shall not exceed the total amount paid for that specific order. No liability for indirect or consequential damages. Not responsible for delays from force majeure, customs holds, incorrect address, or carrier failures. Client is solely responsible for US labeling compliance and import regulations.

🔒 10. Privacy & Data

Client data is used solely for order processing and communication. Not sold to third parties. Design files deleted 90 days after order completion. Clients may request data deletion at any time.

// Questions

Frequently asked

Everything startup founders need to know before their first order.

Why Bangladesh? Is quality good?+
Bangladesh is the world's 2nd largest garment exporter. Our partner factories are BGMEA, GOTS, and OEKO-TEX certified — the same mills supplying H&M, Zara, and Gap. You get equivalent quality at 20–35% lower cost.
How does the 21–25 day delivery work?+
The clock starts after design approval AND 50% deposit clears. Production: 8–12 days. Sea freight: 7–10 days. Customs: 1–2 days. The window includes buffers — reliable, not best-case.
Tell me about the polo shirts and sport jerseys.+
Custom Polo Shirts use 220gsm pique cotton with embroidery or print, $11.50–16 landed. Sport Jerseys are 140gsm dri-fit polyester, perfect for teams and athletic brands, $7–12 landed. Both start at 50 pcs MOQ.
I'm a new brand. Is the 50-pc MOQ safe?+
Absolutely. The Seed tier (50 pcs) is built for brand launches. Test a T-shirt design for ~$625 landed, sell at $24–32 retail, and hit 50%+ margins. Many clients pre-sell on Etsy or Kickstarter before ordering.
Do you handle US customs and import duties?+
We handle all Bangladesh export documentation. US import duties are your responsibility (12–32% by product type, HTS Chapter 61/62). We provide all docs needed for smooth customs clearance.
Can I get samples first?+
Yes. Sample orders (1–5 pcs) at 3× unit price + $35 air freight. Turnaround: 12–15 days. Sample cost credited toward your first bulk order of 50+ pieces.
Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA?+
Yes — FNSKU barcodes, poly bagging, and carton labeling per Amazon requirements available for Growth and Scale orders. Confirm FBA prep needs when ordering.
How do I reach you quickly?+
WhatsApp is the fastest — message us at +1 (443) 862-0065 for same-day replies. You can also email hello@anyprint.co or submit the order form above for a quote.
