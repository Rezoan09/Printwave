"PrintWave" refers to the company operating this platform. "Client" refers to the business or individual placing an order. "Order" means a confirmed purchase of custom printed goods. "Goods" means the custom apparel produced under the Order. "Production Day" means any working day (Mon–Sat) at our Bangladesh facility. "Delivery Window" means the 21–25 calendar day period from Order Confirmation to delivery at the Client's US address.

An order is confirmed only upon receipt of: (a) completed order form with full specs, (b) final approved design files (AI/PDF/PNG at 300dpi min), (c) 50% deposit cleared, (d) written approval of the digital proof. 🏭 5. Production & Quality Control

Standard production: 8–12 production days after confirmed order, approved proof, and cleared deposit. Rush (5–7 days) available at 20% surcharge. Industry tolerance: up to 3% minor defects is accepted; PrintWave replaces or credits items exceeding this. Color variance up to 10–15% from digital mockups is normal. Size tolerance: ±0.5 inches is standard. Pre-shipment photo reports provided for Growth and Scale orders.

🚢 6. Shipping & Delivery
📅 The 21–25 day window starts from Order Confirmation (after design approval + deposit cleared) — not from form submission.

Standard delivery: sea freight to US ports (LA, Miami, NY/Newark, Houston). Air freight available at extra charge (10–14 days). Included: sea freight, origin handling, export docs. NOT included: US customs duties, ISF filing, destination drayage, last-mile delivery (unless stated in quote). Risk transfers to Client at FOB Chittagong/Dhaka. Delays from port congestion, customs holds, or force majeure are outside PrintWave's control.

↩️ 7. Returns & Claims

Claims for defective or incorrect goods must be submitted within 14 calendar days of delivery with photo evidence. Remedies: replacement in a future run or credit equal to defective unit cost. Returns are NOT accepted for: change of mind, slow sales, incorrect size selection by Client, color variation within tolerance, or minor manufacturing characteristics.

🚫 Custom-manufactured goods are non-refundable once production has commenced.

// Questions

Frequently asked

Everything startup founders need to know before their first order.

Why Bangladesh? Is quality good?+
Bangladesh is the world's 2nd largest garment exporter, home to BGMEA-certified factories producing for H&M, Zara, and Gap. Our partner factories are GOTS and OEKO-TEX certified. You get comparable quality at 20–35% lower cost than other Asian sources.
How does 21–25 day delivery work exactly?+
The clock starts after design approval AND 50% deposit cleared. Production takes 8–12 days, sea freight 7–10 days, customs clearance 1–2 days. The 21–25 day window includes buffers — it's reliable, not best-case.
What if I'm a new brand with no customers yet?+
Perfect — our 50-piece Seed tier is designed for brand launches. Test a design for as little as $325–400 landed, sell at $20–28 retail, and still hit 50%+ margins. Many clients pre-sell on Etsy or Kickstarter before ordering.
Do you handle US customs and import duties?+
We handle export documentation from Bangladesh. US import duties are the importer's responsibility. Most apparel from Bangladesh falls under HTS Chapter 61/62 with duties of 12–32% by product type. We provide all docs needed for clearance.
Can I get samples before a full order?+
Yes. Sample orders (1–5 pcs) are available at 3× the unit price plus $35 air freight. Turnaround is 12–15 days. Sample costs are credited toward your first bulk order of 50+ pieces.
Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA?+
Yes, with FBA prep compliance: FNSKU barcodes, poly bagging, carton labeling per Amazon requirements. Available for Growth and Scale tier orders. Confirm FBA prep needs when submitting your order.
What file formats do you accept?+
We prefer AI (Adobe Illustrator) or PDF vector files. For DTF and sublimation, high-res PNG (300dpi+) at print size. We offer a free design check — send your files and we'll flag quality issues before production.
What payment methods are accepted?+
USD bank wire transfer (preferred, no fees), Wise/TransferWise, and PayPal Business. Credit/debit cards via Stripe at 3% surcharge. Crypto available on request for Scale tier orders.
